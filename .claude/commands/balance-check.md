# /balance-check

Analyze the XP, reward, and progression balance for the feature or system in `$ARGUMENTS`.

Evaluate:

1. **XP economy** — are XP awards proportional to workout effort? Is leveling too fast or too slow?
2. **Reward frequency** — how often does the player receive meaningful rewards? Is there a drought?
3. **Level curve** — does the XP required per level feel fair at early, mid, and late game?
4. **Quest balance** — are quest rewards worth the time/effort investment?
5. **Gear progression** — does gear power scale correctly with player level?
6. **Catch-up mechanics** — can a returning player feel progress quickly, or does absence punish too harshly?
7. **Ceiling problem** — is there a point where the player has nothing left to work toward?

Provide specific numbers and suggest concrete adjustments (e.g., "increase quest XP by 20%", "reduce level 10→11 XP requirement from 5000 to 3500").

End with **Next-Level Suggestions** and **Potential Risks / Gaps**.
